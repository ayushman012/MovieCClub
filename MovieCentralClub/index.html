Character Bios

Harry Potter

The Boy Who Lived, Destined to defeat Voldemort. Raised by Muggles, finds true family at Hogwarts.

  • Wand: Holly, Phoenix Feather
  • Speaks Parseltongue
  • Youngest Quidditch Seeker in a Century!
  • Values Friendship Over Fame

Hermione Granger

Brilliant Witch, Top of her class at Hogwarts, & Harry’s best friend. Known for her Quick Thinking & Bravery.

  • Her wand: Vine Wood, Dragon heartstringS
  • Her Favorite Subject: Arithmancy
  • Founded S.P.E.W. (advocating for house elves)
  • Once used a Time-Turner to attend extra classes!
